Account Deletion

How to delete your account

In the app: Settings → "Видалити акаунт" (Delete account). This will permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Request deletion via email

If you cannot access the app, send a request to: trivium.studio.dev@gmail.com

Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What data is deleted

  • Account credentials
  • Voice recordings
  • Diagnostic results and progress
  • AI Coach chat history
  • All cloud-synced data
