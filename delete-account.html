Diqto — Data & Account Deletion

1. Delete your data (keep account)

In the app: Settings → "Очистити дані" (Clear data). This will permanently delete your voice recordings, diagnostic results, progress, and AI Coach chat history while keeping your account active.

2. Delete your account and all data

In the app: Settings → "Видалити акаунт" (Delete account). This will permanently delete your account and all associated data from our servers.

Request via email

If you cannot access the app, send a request to: trivium.studio.dev@gmail.com

Please specify whether you want to delete only your data or your entire account. Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What data is deleted

  • Voice recordings
  • Diagnostic results and progress
  • AI Coach chat history
  • Course progress
  • All cloud-synced data
